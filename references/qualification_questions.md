# Qualification Questions Bank

## Industry-Specific Questions

### HVAC
1. How many service calls do you get per week?
2. How are you currently scheduling appointments?
3. What's your biggest bottleneck: booking, dispatching, or follow-ups?
4. Are you losing leads due to slow response times?

### Plumbing
1. How many emergency calls vs. scheduled jobs?
2. How do you handle after-hours leads?
3. What's your average job size?
4. Are you tracking lead sources?

### Roofing
1. How many estimate requests per week?
2. How long from request to site visit?
3. What's your closing rate on estimates?
4. Are you following up on quotes?

### Landscaping
1. How many crews do you manage?
2. How do you handle seasonal demand spikes?
3. Are you doing recurring work or one-off jobs?
4. What's your average customer value?

### General Contracting
1. What types of projects do you take on?
2. How do you qualify leads before site visits?
3. What's your project pipeline looking like?
4. Are you turning away work due to capacity?

## Budget Qualification

**Direct approach:**
"What budget range are you comfortable with for this type of solution?"

**Softer approach:**
"We offer options from $X to $Y. What works best for your situation?"

## Timeline Qualification

**Urgency check:**
"How soon are you looking to solve this?"
- "Yesterday" = High urgency, route to booking
- "Next few weeks" = Medium urgency, send info + booking
- "Just exploring" = Low urgency, educational route

## Decision Maker Check

"Who else would need to be involved in this decision?"
- If "just me" = Can proceed
- If "partner/manager" = Get all stakeholders on call
